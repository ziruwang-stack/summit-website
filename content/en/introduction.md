RISC-V Summit China 2026 will take place from October 18 to 20, 2026 at the Shenzhen Convention & Exhibition Center (Futian). As an important annual gathering for global open hardware and the RISC-V ecosystem, this Summit will bring together leading scientists, core architects, industry executives, technology developers, and ecosystem partners. The event is expected to attract 3,600+ in-person professional attendees, 450+ exhibitors and research institutions, and reach 800,000+ online industry audiences.

Shenzhen is a global hardware innovation center and a major hub for high-tech industries, with a complete electronics supply chain, rich application scenarios, and an active integrated-circuit innovation ecosystem. Sponsoring this Summit connects your brand, technologies, and solutions directly with core RISC-V ecosystem resources, influential industry partners, and developers, helping accelerate the evolution, adoption, and commercialization of open computing.
